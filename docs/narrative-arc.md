# Narrative arc — Kloten met de broodtrommel

This is a party. A celebration of the breadbin, of C64 demos, of
getting the spark back. Screenfill, intro, greets, and end are
traditional demo parts. The arc runs through interlude, spark,
and coda — but the whole thing is one big lunchbox party.

## Interlude — the confession

```
FOR YEARS NO TIME FOR BREADBIN CODE
```

But here we are. The breadbin booted. The pixels are on screen.

Then the bass drops back in:

```
BUT THEN KLOOT WALKED IN
```

And the party started.

## Spark — A DIGITAL LUNCH EXPERIENCE

This is where the fun begins. Poking registers, chasing the beam,
remembering why C64 demos are the best thing in the world.

## Coda — KLOTEN MET DE BROODTROMMEL

The title card. The trophy. The proof that it happened.

Greets is the party: greeting scene peers, shouting out the lunch-

It's a party. The breadbin is alive. Lunch is served.
